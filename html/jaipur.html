Jal Mahal

Jal Mahal, meaning "Water Palace,"
         is a mesmerizing architectural gem located in the middle of Man Sagar Lake in Jaipur.
            Renowned for its stunning setting amidst the serene lake,
            Jal Mahal appears partially submerged in water during the monsoon season.

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal, located in the heart of Jaipur, India, 
            is a captivating architectural marvel known 
            as the "Palace of Winds."

Jantar Mantar

The site features large-scale instruments, including sundials, 
            giant astrolabes, and massive structures designed for specific
             astronomical calculations. Each instrument is meticulously crafted 
             and strategically positioned to measure time, predict eclipses, and 
             determine the position of stars and planets.
